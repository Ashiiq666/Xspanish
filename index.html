Our Story

Dressing the men of Kozhikode since 2009

X SPANISH began as a single store in Feroke with one belief — that great style should feel personal. Over a decade later, we curate menswear from some of the most loved labels in India and beyond, and walk every customer through it ourselves.

Whether it's a wedding sherwani, a Friday night shirt, or a pair of jeans you'll wear into the ground — we have the fit, the brand, and the eye for detail to make sure you walk out feeling like the best version of yourself.
