Our Story

About X Spanish

Dressing the men of Kozhikode with intention, fit and feeling — since 2009.

Who we are

Menswear, the way it should feel

X Spanish opened its doors in 2009 in the heart of Ramanattukara with a single goal — to make great menswear feel personal again. No piles of clothes you have to dig through. No salespeople reading from a script. Just a team that genuinely loves clothes and wants to find the right fit for you.

Today we run two stores — Ramanattukara and our flagship at Feroke — and stock a carefully chosen line-up of brands. From everyday essentials to wedding-day showstoppers, we've got the rack you'll actually want to shop from.
